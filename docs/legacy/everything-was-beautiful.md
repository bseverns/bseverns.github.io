# Everything Was Beautiful and Nothing Hurt

Recovered photographs establish a completed room-scale installation rather than a proposal. Perforated panels, wood, wall-mounted components, floor elements, and visible cabling occupy a passage so the work is encountered through movement rather than as a single object.

<p class="origin-callout">The archive currently proves the installation and its spatial character. Date, venue, collaborators, and a fuller material list remain intentionally unclaimed until they can be confirmed.</p>
