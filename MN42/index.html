What to do if connect fails
  1. Use Chrome or Edge from `https://` or `http://localhost` for the direct USB path.
  2. If you are unsure, click Check compatibility before reconnecting.
  3. Put the device in configurator mode if the firmware requires it for setup.
  4. Close other serial apps (Arduino Serial Monitor, PlatformIO monitor, bridge scripts).
  5. Unplug and replug the USB cable.
  6. Refresh this page, then click Connect again.
