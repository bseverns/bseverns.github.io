    <h1>Systems &amp; Distribution</h1>
    <p>Infrastructure, deployment, indexing, and governance move the rest of the fleet outward. This pillar is public only where the method can be shown without exposing private topology, credentials, or operational risk.</p>
    <p><a href="/atlas/n/studio-notes/">studio-notes</a> belongs here too, but as a cross-project layer: it distills reusable methods and decisions from the working repos without replacing them.</p>
    <p class="atlas-diagram-note">Several nodes in this pillar are intentionally public-safe excerpts. The point is legible operations, not full infrastructure disclosure.</p>
