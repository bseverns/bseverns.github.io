    <p class="eyebrow">Atlas pillar</p>
    <h1>Tools</h1>
    <p>These are instruments, kits, and analysis layers built so control remains legible. Hardware choices, mappings, latency, failure points, and teaching transfer belong inside the public description rather than behind it.</p>
    <p class="atlas-diagram-note">This pillar is strongest when a tool can show both what it does and how its claims are being tested.</p>

</section>
