    <h1>Learning</h1>
    <p>Learning here means more than course content. Equipment, station cards, policies, maintenance, pacing, and public documentation all teach. Classroom infrastructure is part of the curriculum.</p>
    <p><a href="/atlas/n/studio-notes/">studio-notes</a> connects this pillar to the rest of the fleet by turning project methods into reusable reference and teaching cards.</p>
    <p class="atlas-diagram-note">Several pages in this pillar are intentionally framed as curriculum archives, working prototypes, or public-safe excerpts rather than institutional claims.</p>
